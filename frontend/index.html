About Us

Confidence AI is an innovative application designed to help individuals improve their public speaking and presentation skills. Whether you're preparing for a business presentation, a conference talk, or a class lecture, our app provides real-time feedback on your performance to help you become a more effective and confident speaker.

How it works?

  • Start a Practice Session

    Click on the "Start" button to begin a new practice session. You can choose to either practice with a live audience or record your presentation.

  • Present

    Deliver your presentation as you normally would. Presenter Coach will analyze your voice and facial expressions in real-time, providing feedback on your performance.

  • Receive Instant Feedback

    During your presentation, Presenter Coach provides instant, actionable feedback. You will receive insights on your presentation style, including top emotions detected from your voice and facial expressions.

Key Features

Capture and analyze your presentation in real-time.

Receive immediate, actionable feedback on your presentation style, including top emotions detected from your voice and facial expressions. Post-presentation, get a comprehensive breakdown of your performance, highlighting strengths and areas for improvement.

Understand the emotional impact of your delivery with insights on the top emotions displayed during your presentation.
